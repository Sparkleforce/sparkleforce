About The New California Water Atlas

April 12, 2013. We are launching the Water Rights Atlas as the first in a series of visualizations to help us understand water in California. This initiative will require the cooperation, collaboration and collective knowledge to create more atlas interactives. Read more about the vision.

If you have questions, requests, data or would like to get involved, contact us at info (at) sparkleforce.com. More information about how you can participate will be available in the coming months.
